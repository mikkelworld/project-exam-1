FALCON 9

The Falcon 9 rocket is the world's most reliable and affordable rocket system. Developed by SpaceX in 2011, the Falcon 9 is capable of carrying up to 29.000 kg of cargo into orbit as well as autonomously landing its booster rocket which can then be gathered, refurbished and reused within two weeks. This makes the Falcon 9 the cheapest space launch system in spaceflight history.
