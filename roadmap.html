ROADMAP TO MARS

Mankinds greatest adventure

In the coming years, mankind will begin our journey to become a multiplanetary species.

Scroll down to learn more about how we plan to reach Mars within the next decade.

2020

Starship design and testing

SpaceX will continue testing and finalizing the design of Starship, the vehicle that will eventually transport humans to other planets.

The final design has an estimated payload capacity of over 100 metric tonnes and up to 100 passengers.

2021

First Starship cargo missions

Starship will begin launching into orbit as early as 2021. The spacecraft will be configured for cargo and will lay the foundation for longer missions in the future.

Cargo flights and landings will provide important data for SpaceX before the journey to Mars.

2023

Moon missions

As part of NASA's Gateway project, SpaceX will contribute with cargo missions in 2023.

The Gateway project seeks to establish a long-term presence around the moon to serve as a gateway to the rest of the solar system.

2024

Lunar lander

NASA's Artemis 3 mission aims to land humans on the lunar surface in 2024. SpaceX, along with Blue Origin and Dynetics are competing for the lunar lander contract.

This will be the first time humans have landed on the moon since 1972.

2024 - 2025

First mars missions

SpaceX' Elon Musk has indicated that missions to Mars could take place by as early as 2024 or 2025.

These missions will transport cargo as staging missions before crewed flights take place.

2025 and beyond

Humans on Mars

After multiple successful landings and cargo missions to the martian surface, crewed flights will begin.

Over time, the human colony on Mars will grow. According to Elon Musk, the Mars colony may turn into a city by 2050.
